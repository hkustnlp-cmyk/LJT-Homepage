## About Me

First-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkustnlp-cmyk.github.io/LJT-Homepage/), supervised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://github.com/hkustnlp-cmyk). Graduated from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) (SJTU) in June 2024 with a B.Eng.

My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with particular interests in:
- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability

## Education
